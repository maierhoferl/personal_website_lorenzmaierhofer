Common Questions

FAQ for Professionals

Can I track my patient's progress remotely?

Not yet — the parent dashboard is accessible in-app on the family's device. We recommend asking parents to screenshot the progress report before each session, or reviewing it together on their phone at the visit. A professional portal is on our roadmap.

Is there a clinical version with more controls?

A professional portal with remote access is planned. Contact us at to be notified when it launches.

Is Grimasso appropriate for children with tongue tie post-frenectomy?

Yes. Grimasso's tongue strengthening and mobility exercises are commonly used in pre- and post-frenectomy myofunctional therapy programs. Always integrate with your clinical protocol and post-surgical guidelines.

Does Grimasso replace in-person therapy?

Absolutely not. Grimasso is a homework compliance tool — it supports and extends your clinical work between sessions. It does not assess, diagnose, or replace the professional relationship.
