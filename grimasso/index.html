Exercise Categories

Five Ways to Train

51 scientifically-designed exercises across five categories, each guided by the lovable Grimasso frog.

👅

Tongue Tip

Precise tongue tip movements and placement exercises. Tap, touch, and target the right spot every time!

💪

Tongue Strength

Build real tongue muscle power through fun resistance exercises. Push, press, and grow stronger with each session!

🎯

Tongue Control

Coordination and agility challenges that sharpen tongue control. Wiggle, wave, and master tricky movements!

💧

Swallowing

Proper swallowing pattern exercises that make a real difference. Practice the right technique in a playful way!

🦴

Jaw Muscles

Jaw strengthening and mobility exercises for a solid foundation. Open wide, chew strong, and build control!
