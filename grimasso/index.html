Exercises

Four Fun Categories

Every exercise is designed by speech therapy experts and wrapped in playful fun.

👅

Tongue Exercises

Roll, stretch, and wiggle! Strengthen tongue muscles with hilarious challenges that kids can't stop practicing.

💋

Lip Exercises

Pucker up and blow! Build lip coordination and strength through silly sounds and funny face games.

🤪

Face Exercises

Smile, frown, and make the silliest grimaces! Train all the muscles that help kids speak clearly.

🌬️

Breathing Exercises

Breathe in, breathe out! Calming and fun activities that improve breath control for better speech.
