# Grimasso Features and Benefits Marketing Content

## What
Create compelling marketing copy and content sections showcasing Grimasso's features, benefits, exercise system, gamification, and value proposition for parents and speech therapists.

## Detailed Requirements

- **Features showcase section** highlighting key app capabilities:
  - 51 scientifically-designed tongue exercises across 10 progressive difficulty levels
  - 5 exercise categories: Tongue Tip, Tongue Strength, Tongue Control, Swallowing, Jaw Muscles
  - Animated Grimasso frog mascot providing encouragement and guidance
  - Voice recording for speaking exercises
  - Built-in exercise timers
  - Points system with daily goals
  - Daily streak tracking (3, 7, 30, 60, 100, 200, 365-day badges)
  - 25+ achievement badges to collect
  - PIN-protected parent dashboard
  - Progress tracking with practice calendar
  - Multi-child profile management
  - Configurable screen time limits
  - Customizable reminder notifications

- **Benefits section** targeting parents:
  - Makes speech therapy exercises fun (not a chore)
  - Built on established myofunctional therapy principles
  - Children actually look forward to practicing
  - Safe, kid-friendly, no ads, no third-party tracking
  - Practice anywhere, anytime on iPhone or iPad

- **Target audience callouts**:
  - Children aged 7-12 practicing tongue exercises
  - Parents supporting children's speech development
  - Speech therapists recommending at-home practice tools

- **Social proof / trust section**:
  - App Store rating/review callout
  - "Designed with speech therapy principles" messaging
  - iOS platform badges (iPhone + iPad)

- **Statistics/numbers display** (visually appealing):
  - 51 exercises
  - 10 levels
  - 25+ achievements
  - 5 exercise categories
  - 3 languages supported

- **Call-to-action sections** throughout the page:
  - Primary CTA: Download from App Store
  - App Store link: https://apps.apple.com/ch/app/grimasso/id6758241652

## Constraints

- Content must be accurate to the actual app features (source: ../LogoApp/ codebase)
- Marketing copy must work in all 3 languages (EN/FR/DE) - REQ-006 handles translation
- Must be engaging and not read like a technical spec - this is marketing copy
- Keep sections scannable with icons, numbers, and short paragraphs

## Dependencies

- Depends on: REQ-001 (scaffolding), REQ-002 (visual design for content sections)
- Blocks: REQ-006 (localization needs the English content first as source)

## Builder Guidance

- Certainty level: Firm on features to highlight (they come from the actual app), flexible on exact copy
- Write the English version first as the "source of truth" for translations
- Use playful, parent-friendly language - not overly clinical or technical
- Emphasize the gamification angle - this is what makes Grimasso special
- Consider emoji use in section headers for playfulness (frog, tongue, star, trophy)
