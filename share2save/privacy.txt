Share2Save – Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

Share2Save does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- No account is required to use Share2Save.
- No analytics or tracking libraries are included.
- No data is sent to any server operated by the developer.
- All downloaded files are stored locally on your device.
- The app uses standard HTTPS connections via iOS URLSession for downloading media.
- Downloads are performed using iOS Background URL Session; no data is stored outside your device.

The app includes a compliance notice reminding users that they are responsible for ensuring they have the right to download any content they save.
